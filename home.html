Features

🗺️

Smart Route Planning

Our AI analyzes hundreds of route combinations to find the fastest, most efficient path — accounting for traffic, tolls, and road conditions.

⚡

Real-Time Traffic

Live data from millions of users means you always know what's ahead. Get instant rerouting alerts before delays slow you down.

📍

Multi-Stop Waypoints

Add unlimited stops and let Routly automatically optimize the order to minimize total travel time for all your errands.

🔔

Proactive Alerts

Get notified about accidents, speed cameras, road closures, and weather hazards before they become your problem.

📴

Offline Maps

Download entire regions for offline use. Navigate confidently even when you're deep in a tunnel or far from cell coverage.

🚲

Multi-Mode Travel

Switch between driving, cycling, walking, and transit with one tap. Routly adapts routing rules for every mode of travel.
